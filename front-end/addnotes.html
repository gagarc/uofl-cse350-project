What is addition?

Addition is the combination of two or more numbers to find the total amount, which is called the sum. Its symbol is +.

Example: James has 3 balls and Laura has 2 balls. How many balls do they have together?

Methods to Add 2 Numbers

Using fingers

Small 1-digit numbers can be added using your fingers. Let’s say we have to add 1 and 4. Show 1 finger on one hand and show 4 on the other. Now, count the total number of fingers in both hands. The sum is 5.

Using number line

The number line can be used for larger 1-digit numbers that exceed the fingers in your hands. Look at the following example to add numbers 15 and 3:

  1. Find the bigger number on the line and mark it.
  2. Jump forward as many times as the second number.
  3. Stop when you finish the count and circle the number.

Using column method

Let’s introduce more than 1-digit numbers and understand place value. Numbers can be broken down into groups of ones, tens, hundreds… starting from the right.

Then, the method column consists on writing the numbers one below the other with their digits aligned by place value; and perform the addition on each column.

Look at the following example to add numbers 756 and 384:

  1. Write the numbers one below the other and draw a line under to separate the result.
  2. Start adding the digits in the ones place. Write the sum under the ones column. If the sum is greater that 9, write its ones digit under the column and carry forward its ten digits to the next column.
  3. Follow the same procedure for all the columns, from the right to the left-most column.
  4. When you reach the last column, if there is a carry-on, just place it as the result on the next column.
